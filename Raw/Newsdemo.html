Education in mother tongue is an instrument to fight poverty

Terming education in the mother tongue an empowering ‘instrument’ in ‘fighting poverty’, Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday emphasised on the need to break down linguistic barriers to allow everyone to progress. ...
Without directly referring to the dominance of the English language, the PM pointed out how language has become a barrier for many aspiring youngsters. School districts across the U.S. are increasingly using student test scores to rate the effectiveness of teachers, but a new study found that the scores have less to do with individual teachers.
